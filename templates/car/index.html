A body check of a car focuses on evaluating the structural and cosmetic condition of the vehicle's bodywork to detect issues such as previous damage, poor repairs, rust, or hidden problems. Thickness measurement is a critical part of this process and involves using a paint thickness gauge to assess the uniformity of the paint layer on the car.
Online car checks using the VIN code are an essential tool for buyers, sellers, and car owners to verify the history, condition, and legal status of a vehicle.
